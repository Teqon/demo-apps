Frequently Asked Questions

Clear your doubts about living in Wechi. Do you have questions about living or investing in Wechi? Here you will find clear answers to the most common topics about the project, financing, location, and tax benefits for Dutch buyers.

Master Plan
Wechi is a large-scale development of 134 hectares in Willemstad, Curaçao. Within the next 10 to 15 years, approximately 3,500 to 4,000 homes will be built here, with a focus on sustainability, affordability, and quality of life.
Local Financing
Wechi works actively with local banks to facilitate mortgage opportunities. The following institutions are involved in the project:

  • Maduro & Curiel’s Bank (MCB) – offering a special financing package for Wechi buyers.
  • Banco di Caribe – familiar with the project.
  • Orco Bank – familiar with the project.
  • Fundashon Fiansa Popular – familiar with the project.
Long-term land lease (Erfpacht)
The land in Wechi is issued under long-term land lease by FKP. The annual fee is XCG 1.68 per m² and is based on transparent conditions.
Can I rent out my house?
Yes, both short-term and long-term rentals are allowed. We will help you find tenants!
What are the additional costs?
  • Annual long-term land lease.
  • Service fees of XCG 50 per month for maintenance and security.
  • Transfer and notary costs (details upon request).
Service Costs
  • For the maintenance of shared facilities, each home pays an annual service contribution of XCG 600.
