Website key features

Search Filters

A search bar where you can enter your pick-up and drop-off location, dates and times, and filter by vehicle type, fuel type, and other options.

Vehicle information

Detailed information about each rental car, including make, model, year, engine size, fuel consumption and any additional features such as GPS, Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.

Price information

The rental rate per day or per hour, along with any taxes, fees and insurance options that may apply.

Book now

Customers can reserve a rental car online.

Pick up and drop off instructions

Information about where to pick up and return your rental car, as well as any specific requirements or restrictions that may apply.

Customer Reviews and Ratings

Feedback from other customers who have rented cars from the same company, which can help you make an informed decision.

Contact and support

Information on how to contact the rental company if you have any questions or problems, as well as a frequently asked questions section for common concerns and issues.

Mobile optimization

A mobile-friendly design that lets you search, book and manage your rental car from your smartphone or tablet.

How do we work?

Step 1: Application:

You fill in the application form

Step 2: 50% deposit:

We will send invoice one by one 50% down payment.

Step 3: Intake interview:

Upon receipt of a 50% deposit, we will contact you by telephone to schedule a consultation/intake interview with you.

Step 4: Website content:

You will receive instructions for submitting the content of your website.

Step 5: Build:

After receiving the content, we will start building the website, after which you will receive a link to view your website.

Step 6: Feedback:

You can submit changes 3 times.

Step 7: Delivery and hosting:

Your hosting services are activated. We place your website online on your chosen domain name. You will receive an invoice for your hosting subscription (25,- ANG per month).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a car rental website?

A car rental website is a website that allows users to view, select and rent cars online from a rental company.

Why do car rental companies need a website?

A website is essential for any business, including car rental companies. A website allows customers to view and book cars online, increasing convenience and accessibility. It also helps businesses expand their reach, improve brand visibility, and increase sales.

What features can a car rental website have?

A car rental website should have a user-friendly interface, with features such as easy search and filter options, detailed directions, clear pricing and booking process, secure payment gateway, and responsive design for mobile devices.

How can a web design company help build a car rental website?

A web design company can provide expertise in creating a visually appealing and functional website, tailored to the needs of a car rental company. They can also help integrate third-party systems such as booking engines, payment gateways, and CRM systems.

Can a car rental website be optimized for search engines?

Yes, a car rental website can be optimized for search engines, which can help improve visibility and ranking in search results. We can help optimize the website’s content, structure, and metadata for SEO.

Can a car rental website be integrated with social media?

Yes, a car rental website can be integrated with social media, which can help promote the website, engage customers and improve brand awareness. We can help integrate social media buttons and widgets on the website.

How long does it take to build a car rental website?

The time it takes to build a car rental website can vary depending on the project, required features, and available resources. We can provide an estimate of the timeline after evaluating the project requirements.

How much does it cost to build a car rental website?

The cost of building a car rental website varies per project. We have 3 packages to choose from.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance for a car rental website?

Yes, we can provide ongoing support and maintenance services for a car rental website, including website updates, security patches and technical support.
